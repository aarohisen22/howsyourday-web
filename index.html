How's Your Day

The one thing you need to know about your child's day.

How's Your Day links quietly to your children's school platforms—PowerSchool, Seesaw, and Google Classroom. Each day it looks for new grades, comments, and notices. At the hour you choose, it gives you a single, brief signal about what matters most. There is no scrolling. There is no need to sign in. Only what matters, and nothing more.
