How's Your Day

The one thing you need to know
about your child's school day

How's Your Day links quietly to your children's school platforms—PowerSchool, Seesaw, and Google Classroom. Each day it looks for new grades, comments, and notices. At the hour you choose, it gives you a single, brief signal about what matters most. There is no scrolling. There is no need to sign in. Only what really matters, and nothing more.

How It Works

Three steps to clarity

01

Connect

Link your children's school platforms — PowerSchool, Seesaw, Google Classroom. Credentials are encrypted on your device before they ever leave your phone.

02

Relax

We quietly check for new grades, comments, and notices throughout the day. You don't need to open anything or remember to check.

03

One Signal

At the time you choose, you get one brief, clear signal about what really matters. Only what's important — nothing more.

Privacy

Your child's data is safe

  • School credentials are encrypted on your device before they ever leave your phone.
  • Your data is isolated — only you can access it, enforced at the database level.
  • We never share, sell, or train on your family's information.
  • Raw school data is processed and discarded. Only the daily signal is stored.
